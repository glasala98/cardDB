2024-25 SP Authentic Hockey Checklist Rundown

The 2024-25 SP Authentic Hockey checklist caters to the latest rookie class alongside current stars and retired greats.

Base & Parallel Details

2024-25 SP Authentic Hockey opens with a 100-card base set covering top veterans.

2024-25 SP Authentic Hockey Base Parallels List

  • Limited Red - 1:2 packs
  • Limited Gold - /99
  • Limited Black  - 1/1

Future Watch Details

The Future Watch subset is also part of the base numbering for 2024-25 SP Authentic Hockey, adding 106 names to the rookie lineup.

The main series carries a print run of 999 copies, and these can be unsigned or signed. However, the Limited parallels aren't signed.

Each box averages 10 Limited Red parallels between the base and Future Watch lineups.

2024-25 SP Authentic Hockey Future Watch Parallels List

  • Limited Red - 1:2 packs
  • Limited Blue - /399
  • Limited Green - /199
  • Limited Gold - /99
  • Limited Black  - 1/1

Always a big draw, the Base Future Watch Rookies Autographs (/999) set features signatures from many of the standout rookies.

Some add inscriptions, as well.

Memorabilia enters the chase via Future Watch Auto Patch (/100) and Horizontal Autograph Future Watch Rookies (#'d).

Insert Details

While most of the inserts in 2024-25 SP Authentic Hockey are carryovers, there are still some new options.

One debut is Maximum Performance (1:5 packs), showcasing "star players at the top of their game."

Look for returning inserts like Collaborations (1:5 packs) with teammate pairings, Freshman Focus (1:3 packs) for standout rookies, and Pageantry (1:3 packs), which covers a mix of talent.

Tougher pulls arrive in Future Icons (/399), Holofoils (1:160 packs) and the all-new Legends Holofoil (1:160 packs).

Autograph Details

Although at least one of the autographs per box is from the Future Watch line, 2024-25 SP Authentic Hockey has other possibilities.

This includes Base Limited Auto (1:60), Authentic Moments Autographs (1:480 packs), Chirography (1:160 packs), Curated Signatures (#'d), Limited Auto Material (/100 or less), Marks of Distinction (/25), Pageantry Auto (1:60 packs), Telemetry Autographs (1:120 packs), UD Authentics Autographs (1:60 packs) and UD Authentics Auto Patch (/49 or /25).

A brand fixture, Sign of the Times is back to highlight veterans (1:32 packs) and rookies (1:864 packs).

There are also multi-signed SOTT cards (/25 or less) with two to eight subjects per card.

Signature Handles (1:10 packs) is a new addition to SP Authentic NHL.

UD Bounty Program Details

As has been the case for years, 2024-25 SP Authentic Hockey continues its Upper Deck Bounty program that rewards collectors for completing the entire Spectrum FX insert.

The 91-card set has Spectrum FX Veterans (1:10 packs) and Spectrum FX Future Watch cards. The FW rookie side breaks down into Level 1 (1:20 packs), Level 2 (1:42 packs) and Level 3 (1:160 packs) names.

If you are among the first 50 to finish the set, the award is a Spectrum FX Bounty Award set (9 cards), Gold Spectrum FX set (100 cards) and a surprise bonus. Those falling outside of the first 50 will receive just the nine-card Spectrum FX Bounty Award set.
