2024-25 Upper Deck Synergy Hockey largely keeps the status quo for another season of NHL cards.

Hobby boxes average 15 inserts, eight parallels and one hit in the form of a numbered base card, Base Rookies SP Red parallel, autograph or metal/tech insert.

2024-25 Upper Deck Synergy Hockey Checklist Rundown

Similar to previous years, the 2024-25 Upper Deck Synergy Hockey checklist makes rarity the key element for the base set while working in limited parallels and inserts.

On average, every 16-box case should contain six autographs.

2024-25 Upper Deck Synergy Hockey Base & Parallel Details

Some of the rarest 2024-25 Upper Deck Synergy Hockey cards arrive in the 125-card base set. Base cards for Veterans (#1-60) and Greats (#61-75) have only 15 copies. The Rookies (#76-115), Rookies SPs (#116-125) and Rookies SP Portrait cards are also numbered to 15.

Parallels include Red, falling once per pack, and Purple (/99).

2024-25 Upper Deck Synergy Hockey UD Bounty Details

Tying in the Red parallels, the UD Bounty promotion is another part of 2024-25 Upper Deck Synergy Hockey.

Earn exclusive prizes if you are among the initial 25 to complete and redeem the full set of 125 Base Red parallels (with scratch-off codes). All 25 collectors get the full Base Gold parallel set and the first 10 also earn the Cast for Greatness Gold set.

Please note that only around half of the Base Red parallels have the codes that are required to redeem the cards for this promo. Full details will be available at the Upper Deck Bounty site upon release.

2024-25 Upper Deck Synergy Hockey Insert Details

The Synergy NHL brand is built on inserts and 2024-25 Upper Deck Synergy Hockey is no different. 

Many new choices await collectors, such as Colorways (1:4 packs), a 100-card insert with Neon Yellow (1:10 packs), Neon Blue (/99), Neon Pink (/49), Neon Green (/25) and Gold (1/1) parallels.

Interdimensional (1:128 packs) is a new case hit that highlights top rookies.

The all-new NHL Impressions features Superstars (1:10 packs), Goalies (1:20 packs), Rookies (1:14 packs) and Greats (1:40 packs).

More debuts arrive via #1 Hits (1:16 packs), All-Star Assembly (1:32 packs), All-Synergy Team (1:67 packs), Drop the Hammer (1:40 packs), d2d (1:32 packs), Generators (1:8 packs), Monuments (1:9 packs), New Game (1:6 packs) and Start your Engines (1:8 packs).

Of course, a few inserts from previous years are back for another run. The Rookie Journey series has Draft (1:27 packs), Home (1:27 packs) and Away (1:27 packs) versions. Synergistic Duos also opts for a three-pronged attack with Current Stars (1:23 packs), Star & Great (1:23 packs) and Star & Rookie (1:23 packs) pairings.

Other returning inserts include Cast for Greatness Metal (1:60 packs), StarQuest (1:64 packs) and Waffleboarded (1:45 packs).

2024-25 Upper Deck Synergy Hockey Autograph Details

If you're hunting for signed cards, 2024-25 Upper Deck Synergy Hockey has several limited sets to check out.

Purple Auto parallels extend the Base lineup for select Veterans (/65, /35 or /15), Greats (/35 or /15), Rookies (/99), Rookies SP (/99) and Rookies SP Portraits (/10). There are also Rookies SP Portrait Red parallels (/25).

Look for more autographed cards in Cast for Greatness Signatures (/25), New Game Sygnatures (1:320), NHL Impressions Green Auto (#'d), Rookie Sygnatures (1:120) and Sygnatures (1:160).
