2024-25 Ultimate Collection Hockey Checklist Rundown

The 2024-25 Ultimate Collection Hockey checklist adopts a hit-heavy approach, relying on NHL star and rookie autographs, as well as high-quality swatches.

Base & Insert Details

The 2024-25 Ultimate Collection Hockey series features a 100-card base set, comprising 75 veterans (/149) and 25 greats (/149). Look for Gold (/99), Purple (/35) and Platinum (/5) parallels.

Signed versions include Base Auto (Vets - 1:8 packs; Legends - 1:48 packs), Gold Auto (#'d) and Platinum Auto (1/1) cards. There is also memorabilia in the mix, such as Jumbo Jersey (/199 or /25), Jumbo Patch (/10 or /3), Jumbo Auto Patch (/10 or /3), Jumbo Shield (1/1) and Jumbo Auto Shield Patch (1/1).

In addition, young talent is on display with the Ultimate Introductions (1:5 packs) and Ultimate Phenoms (1:8 packs) inserts. These add Gold (/25) and Platinum (1/1) parallels, along with Auto (Introductions - 1:5 packs; Phenoms - 1:40 packs), Gold Auto (/49), Purple Auto (/10) and Platinum Auto (1/1) cards.

Every box has one base card or insert.

Ultimate Rookies Details

Of course, the Ultimate Rookies line (#'d) remains a focal point of 2024-25 Ultimate Collection Hockey.

These popular rookie cards come in signed and unsigned versions. The chase continues with Ultimate Rookies Jersey (/499), Ultimate Rookies Auto Patch (#'d), Ultimate Rookies Jumbo Auto Patch Variant (/15) and Ultimate Rookies Auto Shield Patch (1/1).

Auto, Relic & Tech Details

2024-25 Ultimate Collection Hockey works in many other hits, as well. Autograph fans have Ultimate Lineup (/5), Ultimate Rookie Signatures (1:8 packs), Ultimate Signatures (1:6), Ultimate Triple Signatures (/15), Ultimate Quad Signatures (/10) and more.

Many autographs add another element to the card, such as an engraved metal piece in Ultimate Emblems Auto (1:8 packs) and Ultimate Emblems Auto Rookies (1:5 packs). These are hard-signed cards.

Ultimate Material Signatures (#'d) boasts on-card content, as well, plus a swatch. The Black Tag version has only five copies.

Memorabilia also pairs with signatures in Rookie Signature Premium Materials (#'d), Ultimate Access Patch Autos (#'d), Ultimate Chapter Jersey Signatures (1:160) and Ultimate Shield Patch Auto (1/1).

Premium relics are another key part of the 2024-25 Ultimate Collection Hockey checklist. The Ultimate Shield Patches (1/1) and Rookie Tandem Shield Patches (/2) lines remain a top chase.

Game-used gear comes in fours with Ultimate Quad Materials Jersey (#'d) and Patch (#'d) cards.

More memorabilia arrives via Debut Threads (#'d), Pro Threads (#'d), Ulti-Mates Jersey (1:7 packs), Ultimate Heroes Patch (#'d) and Ultimate Patches (/10).
