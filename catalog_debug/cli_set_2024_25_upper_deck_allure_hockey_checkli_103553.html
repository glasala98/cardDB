2024-25 Upper Deck Allure Hockey Checklist Rundown

2024-25 Upper Deck Allure Hockey boasts top rookies, new and returning inserts, chase sets and much more for NHL collectors.

2024-25 Upper Deck Allure Hockey Base & Parallel Details

The 2024-25 Upper Deck Allure Hockey checklist totals 100 veteran cards in the base set.

The final 50 cards in the 150-card set cover top rookies. These rookie cards fall once per pack. There are also five cards in the Extended Rookies set.

Each Hobby pack averages one Black Rainbow, Red Rainbow or Orange Slice parallel. Glitter Bomb parallels are only found in Blasters. Other parallels include Gold Glitter Bomb (/199), Purple Diamond (/10) and more.

In addition, short-printed image swaps appear in the Base Photo Variation (1:144 packs) lineup.

2024-25 Upper Deck Allure Hockey Insert Details

From there, 2024-25 Upper Deck Allure Hockey works in multiple inserts.

Across the Pond (1:20 packs) is a new choice that showcases foreign-born players who hail from Europe. Another debuting insert, the next wave of NHL talent is found in Colorful Futures (1:11 packs). Dedicated to the best goalies, Protectors of the Ice (1:17 packs) is also new.

These three inserts add Orange, Green, Blue, Purple, Spectrum Orange (/199), Spectrum Green (/99), Spectrum Blue (/50), Spectrum Purple (/25) and Golden Treasures (1/1) parallels.

Offering a new round of rainbow sets, Color Flow is back for more in 2024-25 Upper Deck Allure Hockey. This time, the overall selection has nearly doubled to highlight 60 players.

The interconnecting series keeps the same five color groupings as in past years, including Red-Orange (1:3 packs), Orange-Yellow (1:4 packs), Yellow-Green (1:5 packs), Green-Blue (1:7 packs) and Blue-Purple (1:13 packs) versions.

On top of that, each tier has serial-numbered Spectrum versions plus the elusive Golden Treasures (1/1) parallel.

More cardboard treasure awaits with the shiny Doubloons (1:72 packs), offering many first-time appearances. These combine with autographs to act as a box hit.

Among the super short prints (SSP), 16-Bit (1:720 packs) goes the old-school, pixelated route, promising a fresh batch of current stars and rookies.

Another SSP is the all-new Expressive Intentions (1:1,440 packs), which showcases art-styled images of 10 superstars.

2024-25 Upper Deck Allure Hockey Autograph Details

If you prefer autographs, 2024-25 Upper Deck Allure Hockey has that covered, too. Look for Base Red Rainbow Auto versions, plus Blue Line (/35), Purple Diamond (/10) and Golden Treasures (1/1) autograph parallels.

Signed inserts include Across the Pond Auto (1:400 packs), Color Flow Full Rainbow Auto (1:24 packs), Colorful Futures Auto (1:240 packs) and Protectors of the Ice Auto (1:360 packs).
