Continuing the storied NHL card brand, 2024-25 O-Pee-Chee Hockey adds another chapter to the Upper Deck OPC lineup.

On average, Hobby boxes should yield 10 parallels, seven inserts and one "rare chase card."

2024-25 O-Pee-Chee Hockey Checklist Rundown

Keeping things more or less the same as in recent years, the 2024-25 O-Pee-Chee Hockey checklist sticks with the normal selection of base cards and inserts.

Collectors also have a chance to claim a top rookie via the Top Rookie Puzzle Bounty (1:96 packs). This annual Hobby program involves collecting and redeeming the nine-card puzzle set for a prize. Per UD, "Those that complete the puzzle will be awarded up to three (3) highly sought-after bounty award cards featuring a top rookie."

2024-25 O-Pee-Chee Hockey Base & Parallel Details

As usual, 2024-25 O-Pee-Chee Hockey contains a 600-card set with 500 base veterans and 100 high-number short prints (2024 All-Star and Marquee Rookie) that combine to fall once per pack.

Variations offer different photos with various themes, such as Black & White Test Proof Variants, Draft Photo Variants, End-of-Game Three Stars Photo Variants, Goal-Scoring Celebration Photo Variants and Helmet-Off Photo Variants.

There are also a few limited parallels, including Blue Border (1:3 packs) and Red Border (1:18 packs). The all-new Orange Border parallels are capped at 25 copies.

2024-25 O-Pee-Chee Hockey Base Parallels List

  • Yellow Border - 1 per pack (Blaster exclusive)
  • Blue Border - 1:3 Hobby/Fat Pack; 1:6 Retail/Blaster
  • Red Border - 1:18 Hobby/Fat Pack; 1:36 Retail/Blaster
  • Orange Border - /25 (Hobby exclusive)
  • Red Border Blank Backs - 1/1 (Hobby exclusive)
  • Printing Plates - 1/1 (Hobby exclusive)

In addition, chase the Hobby-only Rainbow Foil editions of the All-Star and Marquee Rookie cards.

Base All-Star & Marquee Rookie Parallels List

  • Rainbow Foil - /350 (Hobby exclusive)
  • Rainbow Foil Black - /100 (Hobby exclusive)
  • Rainbow Foil Green - /33 (Hobby exclusive)
  • Rainbow Foil Gold - 1/1 (Hobby exclusive)

Another OPC staple is the Retro Variation line for the entire 600-card set. These have Hobby-exclusive Black Border (/100) parallels and Printing Plates (1/1).

2024-25 O-Pee-Chee Hockey Manufactured Patches Details

2024-25 O-Pee-Chee Hockey offers more OPC fixtures via the O-Pee-Chee Patches manufactured relics line.

Look for First Round Pick Draft Locations (1:192 packs), which is new, and Team Logo Update (1:576 packs) cards.

2024-25 O-Pee-Chee Hockey Insert Details

From there, 2024-25 O-Pee-Chee Hockey finishes with a few standard inserts. Only in Hobby boxes, the OPC Playing Cards deck includes 2s to 10s (1:6 packs), Jacks to Kings (1:34 packs), Aces (1:101 packs) and Jokers (1:203 packs).

Another Hobby-only option, the super short print (SSP) 3D Marquee Rookie set supplies a lenticular chase that averages 1:864 packs.

Showcasing a retro style, O-Pee-Chee Premier (1:6 packs) adds new Diamond Rainbow Foilboard parallels, numbered to 75 copies. These rare parallels are only in Hobby boxes.

From there, the chase is on for glow-in-the-dark Glo Pee-Chee cards, plus 2024-25 OPC Platinum Previews inserts. Both of these sets appear randomly in Hobby packs.
