2024-25 O-Pee-Chee Platinum Hockey Checklist Rundown

The 2024-25 O-Pee-Chee Platinum Hockey checklist is packed with shiny Platinum stock featuring NHL stars and Marquee Rookies.

2024-25 O-Pee-Chee Platinum Hockey Base & Parallel Details

Combining veteran and rookie talent, 2024-25 O-Pee-Chee Platinum Hockey delivers 300 base cards. This includes the Marquee Rookie subset for the final 100 cards in the set.

Falling twice per Hobby box, Pink Magma replaces Pink Matte from last season. Other Hobby box finds include six Rainbow parallels and three Sunset parallels. In addition, look for five serial-numbered parallels per Hobby box, numbered to 299 or less, such as the ultra-limited Bioluminescence (/5) and Golden Treasures (1/1).

Fans can find three exclusive Neon Yellow Surge parallels per Blaster.

2024-25 O-Pee-Chee Platinum Hockey Insert Details

Chromium inserts form another key part of the 2024-25 O-Pee-Chee Platinum Hockey checklist.

The 100-card Retro insert (1:2 Hobby; 1:12 Blaster) is back for more with an added RC logo for the rookies. The Blue Luster (/100) parallel is new, replacing Blue Pulsar.

Other returning options include Showmanship (1:4 Hobby; 1:24 Blaster), offering vets and rookies, and Sweet Selections (1:4 Hobby; 1:20 Blaster), for top rookies.

Power Playmakers (1:3 Hobby; 1:14 Blaster) acts as the main debut among the inserts.

2024-25 O-Pee-Chee Platinum Hockey Autograph Details

Turning to the autographs, 2024-25 O-Pee-Chee Platinum Hockey gives collectors some of the season's earliest on-card rookie content.

Led by the top pick in the 2024 NHL Draft (Macklin Celebrini), the popular Rookie Autographs line is hard-signed and packed with top prospects.

Similar to the base set, there are multiple choices among the parallels, like the distinct Sunset (1:120 Hobby) edition and more.

Chase other signatures in Blue Rainbow Auto (1:60 packs), Retro Veterans Red Auto (1:192 packs) and Retro Rookies Red Auto (1:384 packs).

Hobby exclusives include Power Playmakers Rainbow Auto (1:576 packs), Showmanship Rainbow Auto (1:576 packs) and Sweet Selections Rainbow Auto (1:576 packs).
