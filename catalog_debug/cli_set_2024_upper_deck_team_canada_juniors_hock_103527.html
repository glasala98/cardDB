Release Date: October 9, 2024

Stars of the Canadian ranks unite once more for 2024 Upper Deck Team Canada Juniors Hockey.

The Canada-exclusive product plays host to top players from the U18 and U20 World Juniors teams.

On average, Hobby boxes contain three hits that can be autographs or relics.

2024 Upper Deck Team Canada Juniors Hockey Checklist Rundown

As usual, 2024 Upper Deck Team Canada Juniors Hockey sticks with key names at the international level. Unlike past releases, it's just the men's teams this time.

The product boasts the first Team Canada Juniors autographs of Gavin McKenna and Macklin Celebrini.

2024 Upper Deck Team Canada Juniors Hockey Base & Parallel Details

The 2024 Upper Deck Team Canada Juniors Hockey checklist is built around a 150-card base set with three groupings.

The first 69 cards highlight various subjects from the U18 and U20 squads, with the best players saved for Pride of the Program (#70-100).

From there, the chase is on for Patterned Foilboard versions (4 per box) and the all-new Outburst editions (1 per box), while Blasters have exclusive Rose Gold Foil (1:2 packs) and Blue Patterned Foilboard (1:120 packs) parallels. Collectors can also find debuting Metal Variation (1:100 packs) and Plexi Variation (1:255 packs) cards.

2024 Upper Deck Team Canada Juniors Hockey Base Parallels List (#1-100)

  • Red Foil - 1 per pack (Hobby/ePack)
  • Rose Gold Foil - 1:2 packs (Blaster)
  • Patterned Foilboard - 1:5 packs  (Hobby/ePack)
  • Outburst - 1:15 packs (Hobby/ePack)
  • Purple Patterned Foilboard - 1:15 packs (Hobby/ePack)
  • Clear Cut - 1:40 packs (Hobby/ePack)
  • Blue Patterned Foilboard - 1:120 packs (Blaster)
  • Metal Variation - 1:100 packs (Hobby/ePack)
  • Plexi Variation - 1:255 packs (Hobby/ePack)
  • Exclusives - /100 (Hobby/ePack)
  • Outburst Red - /25 (Hobby/ePack)
  • Gold Patterned Foilboard - /25 (Hobby/ePack)
  • High Gloss - /10 (Hobby/ePack)
  • Outburst Gold - 1/1 (Hobby/ePack)

Program of Excellence (1:4 packs) features the final 50 cards in the series. The POE cards have only Clear Cut (1:80 packs), Exclusives (/100), High Gloss (/10) and Finite (1/1) parallels.

2024 Upper Deck Team Canada Juniors Hockey Insert Details

In addition, a fresh assortment of inserts awaits fans in 2024 Upper Deck Team Canada Juniors Hockey.

New choices include Bound for Greatness (1:60 packs) and Team Canada Logo Metal Die-Cut (1:255 packs).

Also appearing for the first time, Future Stardom (1:30 packs) displays acetate stock.

Prospectus Momentous (1:10 packs) is a returning option that focuses on past and future draft picks. These have seven Electric parallels numbered to 399 or less.

Averaging 1:180 packs, the Team Canada FX short print is also back for more.

2024 Upper Deck Team Canada Juniors Hockey Autograph & Relic Details

That leaves just the autographs and relics in the 2024 Upper Deck Team Canada Juniors Hockey checklist.

Most come from the base lineup, including Jersey (1:6 packs), Signatures (1:160 packs) and Auto Patch (/175 or /125).

On top of that, the Pride of the Program subset adds Jersey (1:60 packs), Signatures (1:640 packs) and Auto Patch (/25) cards for select names, while Program of Excellence has limited Auto (1:120) versions.

Prospectus Momentous rounds out the hits with Auto (/15) and Black Auto (1/1) parallels.

Estimated Release Date: October 9, 2024

Hobby Boxes (on average)

  • 4 Autograph, Memorabilia AND/OR Tech Cards
  • 15 Base Red Foil Parallels
  • 4 Clear Cut AND/OR #’d Non-Auto/Mem Parallels
  • 4 Base Patterned Foilboard and/or Purple Patterned Foilboard Parallels
  • 1 Base Outburst Parallel
  • 6 Program of Excellence AND/OR Prospectus Momentous Cards

Blaster Boxes (on average)

  • 3 Rose Gold Foil Parallels
  • 2 Program of Excellence Cards
  • 1 Prospectus Momentous Insert
