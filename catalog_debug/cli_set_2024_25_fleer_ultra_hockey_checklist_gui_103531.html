Release Date: November 19, 2025

Brands

2024-25 Fleer Ultra Hockey is back for collectors as an Upper Deck ePack exclusive. While the trading cards are initially exclusive to the online platform, many pulls can be redeemed for physical cards.

The eight-card packs sell for $11.99, the 15-pack boxes are $173.99 and the 10-box cases go for $1,679.99.

2024-25 Fleer Ultra Hockey Checklist Rundown

The 2024-25 Fleer Ultra Hockey checklist mixes digital-only cards with digital cards that represent physical cards.

These physical cards can be obtained via Check Out My Cards (COMC) or The Authority.

Base & Parallel Details

Referred to as "fractional" by UD, the 2024-25 Fleer Ultra Hockey base set is available only in digital form. However, this set, including 50 rookies, has physical parallels that can be pulled and ePack Achievements that can be earned by completing tasks.

In fact, every pack averages one physical Silver Foil parallel.

2024-25 Fleer Ultra Hockey Base Parallels List

  • Silver Foil - 1 per pack
  • Blue Foil - /399
  • Red Foil - /94
  • Orange Foil - /25
  • Gold Spectrum Foil - 1/1
  • Printing Plates - 1/1

The digital base cards can also be used to unlock exclusive parallels. Collect five base cards of the same player to redeem an exclusive Rainbow Foil parallel. In addition, five Rainbow Foil parallels make one Patterned Foil parallel, and five Patterned Foil parallels can be combined to yield one Gold Patterned Foil Parallel.

Insert Details

As for the inserts, 2024-25 Fleer Ultra Hockey features just as many choices as a traditional release. These are all good for physical cards.

Several are common pulls that appear in each box, with most adding limited parallels.

  • ExCELLYng - 1:7 packs
  • Medallions - 1:2 packs
  • Photo Effex - 1:13 packs
  • Puckheads - 1:5 packs
  • Ultra Power - 1:8 packs
  • Ultra Sensations - 1:13 packs
  • Ultra Stars - 1:15 packs
  • Ultra Team - 1:10 packs
  • Ultrasonic - 1:7 packs

Less common pulls include Net Workers (1:25 packs), Rising Stars (1:50 packs) and Takeit2.net (1:38 packs).

The chase is on for super short prints (SSP) in Fame Game (1:150 packs), Hot Hands (1:448 packs), Thunderclap (1:150 packs) and Uncorked (1:226 packs).

Autograph Details

Finally, autographs are possible in 2024-25 Fleer Ultra Hockey, but the odds are fairly tough.

The main series has Base Rainbow Foil Autographs for the vets (1:190 packs) and rookies (1:79 packs).

Limited signed inserts include ExCELLYng Autographs (1:487 packs), Puckheads Autographs (1:269 packs) and Ultrasonic Autographs (1:1,329 packs).
