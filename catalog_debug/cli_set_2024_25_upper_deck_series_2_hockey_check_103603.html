2024-25 Upper Deck Series 2 Hockey continues the flagship set for the NHL with new Young Guns and inserts to collect.

Of course, Series 2 is the bridge between Series 1 and Extended Series.

2024-25 Upper Deck Series 2 Hockey Checklist Rundown

Rookies dominate the 2024-25 Upper Deck Series 2 Hockey checklist, especially the newest Young Guns.

Macklin Celebrini and Matvei Michkov headline the latest YG lineup.

2024-25 Upper Deck Series 2 Hockey Base & Parallel Details

2024-25 Upper Deck Series 2 Hockey adds to the base set numbering from Series 1, going from card #251 to #450. This includes two checklists.

The parallel lineup keeps the same approach as Series 1, mixing the newer Outburst editions, falling once per Hobby box, with old favorites like Exclusives (/100) and High Gloss (/10). The Hobby-only Clear Cut acetate parallels are also back.

2024-25 Upper Deck Series 2 Hockey Base Parallels

  • Outburst Silver - 1:15 packs
  • Clear Cut - 1:48 (Hobby exclusive)
  • Deluxe - /250
  • Exclusives - /100
  • Outburst Red - /25
  • High Gloss - /10
  • Outburst Gold - 1/1

Back for more, the Swagnificent Variation series highlights top names in their pre-game suit attire. There's also Snow Spray Autographs, with added signatures from nine base subjects.

2024-25 Upper Deck Series 2 Young Guns & Parallel Details

Next, 2024-25 Upper Deck Series 2 Hockey turns to the Young Guns subset, which occupies cards #451 to #500 in the overall lineup.

This includes four dual-player Teammates cards.

The fifth dual, and final card in base set, is a checklist with Celebrini and Michkov.

In addition, the 50-card YG series has the same parallels as the standard base lineup.

2024-25 Upper Deck Series 2 Hockey Young Guns Parallels

  • Outburst Silver - 1:60 packs
  • Clear Cut - 1:144 (Hobby exclusive)
  • Deluxe - /250
  • Exclusives - /100
  • Outburst Red - /25
  • High Gloss - /10
  • Outburst Gold - 1/1

2024-25 Upper Deck Series 2 Hockey Insert Details

As usual, inserts account for the bulk of 2024-25 Upper Deck Series 2 Hockey. Many of the sets, such as Dazzlers, Fluorescence and Population Count (1,000 copies or less), should be very familiar to collectors by now.

UD Portraits also extends the Series 1 insert to offer 30 new cards.

Switching the focus back to YG cards, Series 2 provides round two of Young Guns Renewed (1:4 packs). These put a new spin on classic rookie cards with the talent of artist Luke The Cardist. Unlike the base Young Guns, the artistic cards only have Exclusives (/100), High Gloss (/10) and Printing Plate (1/1) parallels.

On top of that, the popular UD Canvas line is another heavily collected staple of the flagship brand. The continuation series includes base UD Canvas (1:4 packs), UD Canvas Program of Excellence (1:96 packs), UD Canvas Retired (1:96 packs) and UD Canvas Young Guns (1:24 packs). Each subset has Black & White editions and Printing Plates (1/1).

As for the rest of the 2024-25 Upper Deck Series 2 Hockey checklist, the remaining content is new.

Resembling a band poster, On Tour (1:5 packs) takes the NHL show on the road to highlight teams and players.

Welcome Aboard (1:5 packs) celebrates some of the top '24-25 rookies with a nautical theme.

Other new inserts arrive via Bang For Your Puck (1:7 packs), ElectriCITY (1:5 packs), On a Heater (1:10 packs), Ooh La La (1:5 packs) and Playlist (1:5 packs). Most of these sets add Speckle, Black, Red (/99) and Gold (/10) parallels.

Be on the lookout for super short prints (SSP) in Day With The Cup, Fanimation, Incarnations and Outburst Retro Young Guns.
