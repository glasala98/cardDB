2024-25 Topps NHL Sticker Collection Hockey highlights the stars of the league with a massive selection of stickers. Each pack has one foil sticker card.

Display boxes are $49.99 and the sticker albums are $23.99.

The albums can be used to house the stickers across four page covers and 96 inner pages. There is also a free sticker sheet that includes four exclusive stickers and six non-exclusives.

2024-25 Topps NHL Sticker Collection Hockey Set Details

Digging deep, the 2024-25 Topps NHL Sticker Collection Hockey checklist covers all 32 NHL teams in the 544-sticker base set.

Limited parallels include Blue Foil (/100), Red Foil (/10) and Gold Foil (1/1).

2024-25 Topps NHL Sticker Collection Hockey Base Parallels List & Odds

  • Blue Foil - /100 - 1:25 Display; 1:26 Gravity Feed; 1:28 Gravity Feed Grosnor.
  • Red Foil - /10 - 1:250 Display; 1:259 Gravity Feed; 1:272 Gravity Feed Grosnor.
  • Gold Foil - 1/1 - 1:2,499 Display; 1:2,504 Gravity Feed; 1:2,733 Gravity Feed Grosnor.

Falling once per pack, the Foil editions come from several lines:

  • Base Players & Mascots Foil
  • Base Team Logo Foil
  • Covet the Cup Foil
  • NHL Hardware Foil
  • NHL Three Stars of the Month Foil
  • One Time Foil
  • Stanley Cup Trophy Puzzle Foil

Other themed subsets pay tribute to past Topps designs, notable NHL moments and more.

  • 1974-75 Topps NHL All-Stars
  • 1979-80 Topps NHL Rookies
  • 2024 NHL Captains
  • NHL All-Star Weekend
  • Post to Post
  • Sweater Weather
  • Rookie Showcase
  • The Great Outdoors
  • Top Shelf Talents

Once you account for all the subsets, that pushes the overall 2024-25 Topps NHL Sticker Collection Hockey checklist to 800 stickers.

This means that collectors have a lot of work ahead of them if they intend to complete the entire set.
