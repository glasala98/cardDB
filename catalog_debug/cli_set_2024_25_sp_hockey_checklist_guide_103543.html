Blaster boxes ($29.99) should yield eight Blue parallels.

2024-25 SP Hockey Checklist Rundown

Not a lot has changed for 2024-25 SP Hockey, which still relies on the SP Rookie Authentics and Authentic Profiles lines.

2024-25 Hockey Base & Parallel Details

The 2024-25 SP Hockey checklist includes 100 veterans in the base set.

The base set also adds 40 SP Rookie Authentics cards, numbered to 1,299 copies.

Overall, the Blue parallels should combine to fall once per pack.

The rookie rainbow has decreased with a much smaller Red parallel print run and the removal of the Green Tint Patterned Foilboard and Purple parallels.

2024-25 SP Hockey SP Rookie Authentics Parallels List & Odds

  • Blue - 1 per pack
  • Patterned Foilboard - 1:48 packs
  • Gold Patterned Foilboard - 1:200 packs
  • Red - /99
  • Green - /25
  • Black - 1/1

2024-25 SP Hockey Insert Details

In addition, the Authentic Profiles insert is back in 2024-25 SP Hockey, offering 1,299 copies per base insert. The parallel selection is the same as the SP Rookie Authentics, but with lower totals for the Red (/49) and Green (/10) versions.

2024-25 SP Hockey SP Authentic Profiles Parallels List & Odds

  • Blue - 1:5 packs
  • Patterned Foilboard - 1:192 packs
  • Gold Patterned Foilboard - 1:800 packs
  • Red - /49
  • Green - /10
  • Black - 1/1

2024-25 SP Hockey Autograph & Relic Details

As for the hits, 2024-25 SP Hockey maintains the usual selection. The SP Rookie Authentics subset has Signatures (1:480 packs) cards with Gold (/25) parallels.

Veteran names are saved for Authentic Profiles Signatures (1:2,880 packs) and Authentic Signatures (1:1,600 packs).

Lastly, the game-worn memorabilia chase includes Authentic Profiles Jersey (1:640 packs) and SP Rookie Authentics Jersey (1:40 packs) relics.

Estimated Release Date: February 19, 2025

  • 4 Rookie Authentics Blue Parallels
  • 1 Authentic Profiles Blue Parallel
