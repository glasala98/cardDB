2024-25 Upper Deck Credentials Hockey Checklist Rundown

The 2024-25 Upper Deck Credentials Hockey checklist is built around the rookie-filled Debut Ticket Access set and the hard-signed content.

Base & Parallel Details

2024-25 Upper Deck Credentials Hockey features a base set anchored by 100 veterans with multiple parallels.

Insert Details

Inserts remain plentiful for 2024-25 Upper Deck Credentials Hockey, with a mixture of new content to go along with returning lines.

The newness opens with Frozen Fight, showcasing a face-off between two stars.

Milestone Pucks (1:7 packs) pays tribute to a notable achievement with a shot of the player holding the puck in question.

Honoring hat tricks, That's a Hatty (1:5 packs) shows the celebration after the third goal is notched.

There's also Cheered to the Rafters (1:5 packs), Front Row Talent (1:3 packs) and Jumping on the Ice (1:4 packs).

These six sets include Red (/299) and Green (/49) parallels.

Among the debuts in 2024-25 Upper Deck Credentials Hockey, the Bobbleheads line comes in three groupings: Level 1 (750 copies), Level 2 (250 copies) and Level 3 (100 copies).

Hard to miss, Repetitive Head Shirts is another new set. These are limited to 250 copies per card.

The distinct designs continue with Championship Rings (100 copies), Hawaiian Print Shirts (100 copies) and Jerseys (500 copies). In addition to the announced print run, each die-cut has a Blue Back parallel, serial-numbered to 25 copies.

Look for returning inserts via Acetate Debut Ticket Access (/199), Acetate Ticket Access (/99), Acetate Retro Ticket Access (1:240 packs), Acetate Retro Ticket Access Rookies (1:120 packs), Fleer Pro Visions (1:300 packs), Fleer Pro Visions Legends (1:600 packs), Speed of the Game Stars (1:32 packs) and Speed of the Game Rookies (1:11 packs).

Autograph Details

Headlining the 2024-25 Upper Deck Credentials Hockey checklist, on-card autographs are a huge draw for the release once more.

Select veterans have Base Auto (1:36 packs), Base Purple Auto (/10) and Base Black Auto (1/1) cards.

There are also Debut Ticket Access Autograph rookies (/299 or /199) with Yellow (/85), Green (/25), Purple (/10) and Black (1/1) parallels.

The Horizontal Debut Ticket Access Autograph rookies (/99) add Purple (/15) and Black (1/1) parallels.

Throwback styles are saved for Retro Ticket Access Auto (1:160 packs) and Retro Ticket Access Auto Rookies (1:48 packs), adding Gold (/5) parallels.

Appealing to the acetate fans, the signed tickets continue in Acetate Debut Ticket Access Auto (/99), Acetate Ticket Access Auto (/99 or less), Acetate Retro Ticket Access Auto (/49 or less) and Acetate Retro Ticket Access Rookies Auto (/25).

Chase more signed inserts in Cheered to the Rafters Auto (1:400 packs), Front Row Talent Auto (1:340 packs), Frozen Fight Auto (1:900 packs), Jumping on the Ice Auto (1:340 packs), Milestone Pucks Auto (1:900 packs) and That's a Hatty Auto (1:900 packs).

The Arena Experience Autographs set moves beyond the ice to cover broadcasters, musicians and other on-air talent.

UDA Signed Memorabilia Details

Another new chase for 2024-25 Upper Deck Credentials Hockey is the UDA Autographed Hockey Jersey Redemption series.

A total of 10 cards are divided among four players, with each redemption earning a signed jersey from UDA.

  • Alexis Lafrenière - New York Rangers (3 jerseys)
  • Connor McDavid - Edmonton Oilers (2 jerseys)
  • Nick Suzuki - Montreal Canadiens (3 jerseys)
  • Shane Wright - Seattle Kraken (2 jerseys)
