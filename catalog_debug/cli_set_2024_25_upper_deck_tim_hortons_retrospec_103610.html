Brands

2024-25 Upper Deck Tim Hortons Retrospective Rookies Hockey gives NHL and PWHL fans a new promotional set to enjoy at their local Tim Hortons location in Canada.

Just like UD has the popular Tim Hortons set in the fall, collectors have come to expect the annual spring edition, as well.

As supplies last, the packs are sold at participating locations in Canada for $1.50 (CAD) with a beverage purchase. Standard pack prices are $1.99 without a drink. There's also an official binder for $19.99.

  • Review other 2024-25 Hockey Card Guides.
  • Check out the 2024-25 Upper Deck Tim Hortons Hockey Checklist Guide.

2024-25 Upper Deck Tim Hortons Retrospective Rookies Hockey Base & Insert Details

Featuring both NHL and PWHL stars, the 2024-25 Upper Deck Tim Hortons Retrospective Rookies Hockey checklist offers 100 cards in the base set. The dual focus highlights a player's past and present, starting with the rookie season. Keeping to tradition, these display French text alongside the English.

There are also multiple insert sets in play. Among the most common insert choices are Celebrated Prodigies (1:6 packs), In the Books (1:4 packs), Initial Strides (1:3 packs) and Take a Bow (1:8 packs). Averaging 1:24 packs, Starting Success offers a mix of talent.

The rarest insert is Dynamic Debuts (1:100 packs), a five-card set with some big names, like Crosby, Lemieux and Ovechkin. This short-printed series also showcases the top pick in the 2023 PWHL Draft (Taylor Heise) and star goalkeeper Igor Shesterkin.

2024-25 Upper Deck Tim Hortons Retrospective Rookies Hockey Autograph Details

On top of that, the 2024-25 Upper Deck Tim Hortons Retrospective Rookies Hockey checklist supplies autograph redemptions at very tough odds.

Falling 1:7,200 packs, the Debut Autographs line contains 16 players. There's also the Debut Season Autograph set for Nathan MacKinnon (1:172,224 packs) and Sidney Crosby (1:57,408 packs).

Other possible prizes include Free Coffee or Tea (1:173 packs; 25,000 total), Free Classic Doughnut or Dream Donut (1:173 packs; 25,000 total), $50 Tim Hortons Gift Cards (1:43,056 packs; 100 total) and Framed Autographed Jersey (1:430,560 packs; 10 total).
