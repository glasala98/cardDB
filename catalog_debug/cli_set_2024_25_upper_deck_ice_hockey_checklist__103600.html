2024-25 Upper Deck Ice Hockey Checklist Rundown

As usual, the 2024-25 Upper Deck Ice Hockey checklist works in a mix of acetate cards, on-card content and Exquisite Collection cards.

While the 2023-24 UD Ice release had two Ice Premieres per box, 2024-25 increases that rookie total to three per box.

2024-25 Upper Deck Ice Hockey Base & Ice Premieres Details

2024-25 Upper Deck Ice Hockey keeps its 150-card base set featuring 100 veterans and 50 rookies.

Each pack should have one base rookie and one base Emerald parallel.

2024-25 Upper Deck Ice Hockey Base Parallels List

Base Set 

  • Emerald (Base - 1:2; Rookie - 1:3)
  • Purple (Base - 1:6; Rookie - 1:12)
  • Greyscale - /99
  • Black - 1/1

Acetate stock makes an appearance in the heavily collected Ice Premieres subset. This 100-card series has five tiered rookie groupings that go from 999 copies to 99 copies per player. The Ice Premieres base cards and parallels combine to yield three per box.

2024-25 Upper Deck Ice Hockey Ice Premieres Rundown

Ice Premieres 

  • Level 5  - /999 (#151-190)
  • Level 4 - /799 (#191-215)
  • Level 3 - /499 (#216-230)
  • Level 2 - /249 (#231-240)
  • Level 1 - /99 (#241-250)

Ice Premieres Parallels

  • Blue - /399
  • Emerald Green - /199
  • Gold - /25
  • Black - 1/1

2024-25 Upper Deck Ice Hockey Insert Details

Shifting to the inserts, 2024-25 Upper Deck Ice Hockey supplies another batch of Sub Zero Stars (1:32 packs), Sub Zero Rookies (/799 or /599) and Sub Zero Rookie Variants (1:500 packs).

Frozen in Ice (1:24 packs) is also back as the brand's designated rip cards. These hold Ice Premieres Minis acetate cards.

Those wanting something new to collect in 2024-25 Upper Deck Ice Hockey can check out Arcticulates (1:9 packs) and Arcticulates Rookies (1:20 packs). The foilboard set has an Arctic theme and adds Gold (/99) and Black (/10) parallels.

SP Ice (or Spice) is another debut. The colorful lineup covers 25 stars and rookies with four different versions: Level 1 (1:48 packs), Level 2 (1:96 packs), Level 3 (1:144 packs), and Level 4 (1:288 packs). The "levels of heat" each have a different color and rarity, including Mild, Medium, Hot and Fire.

Be on the lookout for brand new short prints via Go to Work (1:576 packs) and Ice Shelves (1:576 packs).

World Junior Championship (/99) is another rare insert.

2024-25 Upper Deck Ice Hockey Autograph & Relic Details

In addition, plenty of hits await in the 2024-25 Upper Deck Ice Hockey checklist.

The base set has Jersey (1:96 packs), Rookies Jersey (1:96 packs), White Snowflake Auto (1:288 packs) and Rookies White Snowflake (1:216 packs) cards, plus limited Gold Patch and Gold Auto Patch versions.

The Ice Premieres line offers hits, as well, such as Ice Premieres Auto (/199 or /99), Ice Premieres Jersey (1:60), Ice Premieres Patch (/25) and Ice Premieres Autographed Patch (/10).

Other signed cards in 2024-25 Upper Deck Ice Hockey include Glacial Graphs (1:144 packs) with Black parallels.

Young talent headlines Sub Zero Rookies Blue Auto (1:960 packs) and Sub Zero Rookie Variants Green Auto (/25).

The Go to Work Auto and Ice Shelves Auto cards build on the new inserts with rare autographed editions.

2024-25 Upper Deck Exquisite Collection Hockey Details

However, that's not all for 2024-25 Upper Deck Ice Hockey as 2024-25 Exquisite Collection Hockey supplies more cards to chase.

Numbered to 299 copies or less, the Exquisite Rookies series is joined by Exquisite Crystal (/25), a new plexi offering.

Autographs include Exquisite Collection Rookie Signatures (/99) and Exquisite Collection Rookies Refined Signatures (/50), which is new.
