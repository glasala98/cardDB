  2. Hockey Cards

Release Date: September 17, 2025

Brands

Clicking any links and making a purchase can result in this site earning a commission, including the eBay Partner Network and others.

Last issued for the 2022-23 season, 2024-25 Upper Deck Stature Hockey returns to NHL collectors with a double release featuring '23-24 and '24-25 cards.

Hobby boxes average one hit that can be an autograph, relic or auto relic. This is a change from the '22-23 product that had one autograph or auto relic per box.

  • Review other 2024-25 Hockey Card Guides.
  • 2025-26 Upper Deck Stature Hockey Checklist Guide.

2024-25 Upper Deck Stature Hockey Checklist Rundown

With two seasons packed into the same release, the 2024-25 Upper Deck Stature Hockey checklist offers fans a recap of two different rookie classes.

Base & Parallel Details

Promising "elegantly-designed, thick premium stock cards," 2024-25 Upper Deck Stature Hockey spreads the coverage evenly to highlight '23-24 and '24-25.

Look for 50 cards per year and 100 total cards in the overall breakdown.

  • 2023-24 Base Veterans (#1-25; 1:2)
  • 2023-24 Base Legends (#26-30; /1,199)
  • 2023-24 Base Rookies (#31-50; (/999)
  • 2024-25 Base Veterans (#1-25; 1:2)
  • 2024-25 Base Legends (#26-30; /1,199)
  • 2024-25 Base Rookies (#31-50; /999)

Each box has one veteran card and one serial-numbered base legend or rookie.

There are also two numbered parallels per box.

2024-25 Upper Deck Stature Hockey Base Parallels List

  • Green - /375
  • Red - /249
  • Orange - /199
  • Blue - /99
  • Gold - /50
  • Purple - /25
  • Black - /10

Autograph & Relic Details

Relying on names from the double base series, 2024-25 Upper Deck Stature Hockey works in a selection of hard-signed autographs and autographed relics.

This includes base Auto cards and limited parallels.

2024-25 Upper Deck Stature Hockey Base Auto Rundown

  • Auto - 1:3
  • Green Auto - /199
  • Red Auto - /99
  • Orange Auto - /50
  • Blue Auto - /25
  • Gold Auto - /10
  • Purple Auto - /5
  • Black Auto - /3

Similarly, there are multiple Auto Patch editions with premium versions.

As seen with the rookie cards of Luke Hughes and Lane Hutson, there is a clear design difference between 2023-24 (shown above) and 2024-25 (shown below).

  • Auto Patch - /49
  • Green Auto Patch - /35

  • Orange Auto Premium Memorabilia - /15
  • Blue Auto Premium Memorabilia - /10
  • Gold Auto Premium Memorabilia - /5
  • Purple Auto Premium Memorabilia - /3
  • Black Auto Premium Memorabilia - 1/1

Numbered to 49 or less, the unsigned memorabilia highlights the Base Veterans lineup.

2024-25 Upper Deck Stature Hockey Base Veterans Relic Rundown

  • Patch - /49
  • Green Patch - /35
  • Red Patch - /25
  • Orange Premium Memorabilia - /15
  • Blue Premium Memorabilia - /10
  • Gold Premium Memorabilia - /5

All memorabilia cards (signed and unsigned) are numbered.

There are also additional autograph updates from the 2019-20, 2020-21, 2021-22 and 2022-23 sets.
