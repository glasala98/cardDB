2024-25 Upper Deck CHL Hockey covers the rising talent of the developmental league, with a focus on top prospects.

Hobby boxes average 12 inserts, at least two numbered parallels, and one hit that can be an autograph or Outburst parallel.

The sixty-team Canadian Hockey League (CHL) includes the Ontario Hockey League (OHL), Quebec Maritimes Junior Hockey League (QMJHL) and Western Hockey League (WHL).

2024-25 Upper Deck CHL Hockey Checklist Rundown

The 2024-25 Upper Deck CHL Hockey checklist is packed with potential stars of the NHL.

Per UD, "Over 150 CHL players were included in the NHL Central Scouting’s Preliminary Players to Watch list for the 2025 NHL Draft."

2024-25 Upper Deck CHL Hockey Base & Parallel Details

Up first, a large set awaits collectors of 2024-25 Upper Deck CHL Hockey. This includes 300 Base, 50 Base Stars and 50 Base Star Rookie cards. The short-printed Stars and Star Rookie subsets combine to average one card per pack.

Parallels mirror the flagship approach with UD classics, like Exclusives (/100) and High Gloss (/10), alongside Outburst parallels for the high series. The parallel lineup is also slanted to the short prints (cards #301-400), with seven choices compared to just three parallels for the first 300 cards.

With the one-of-one Printing Plates, some of these have signatures.

2024-25 Upper Deck CHL Hockey Base Parallels List

Base Parallels (#1-300)

  • Exclusives - /100 (Hobby/ePack)
  • High Gloss - /10 (Hobby/ePack)
  • Printing Plates - 1/1  (Hobby/ePack)

Base Stars / Star Rookies Parallels (#301-400)

  • Green - 1:7 packs (Blaster exclusive)
  • Outburst - 1:24 packs (Hobby/ePack)
  • Exclusives - /100 (Hobby/ePack)
  • Outburst Red - /25 (Hobby/ePack)
  • High Gloss - /10 (Hobby/ePack)
  • Outburst Gold - 1/1 (Hobby/ePack)
  • Printing Plates - 1/1 (Hobby/ePack)

2024-25 Upper Deck CHL Hockey Insert Details

As for the inserts, the selection in 2024-25 Upper Deck CHL Hockey is mostly new offerings.

Highlighting top picks from the 2024 drafts, CHL First Draft (1:9 Hobby/ePack; 1:16 Blaster) is the lone carryover.

Debuts include In the Lab (1:4 Hobby/ePack; 1:7 Blaster), showcasing stars in the making, and Prominent Combos (1:5 Hobby/ePack; 1:9 Blaster), which covers top teammate pairings.

Another new insert line is That's a Rocket (1:5 Hobby/ePack; 1:9 Blaster), dedicated to those with a knack for scoring goals.

The inserts each add Red (/125), Blue (/25) and Black (1/1) Foilboard parallels.

All inserts combine to yield nine cards per Hobby box.

2024-25 Upper Deck CHL Hockey Autograph Details

From there, the 2024-25 Upper Deck CHL Hockey checklist features a dose of autographs.

Top names arrive in the Base Auto (1:48 Hobby/ePack/Blaster) and Base Stars & Star Rookies Auto (1:48 Hobby/ePack/Blaster) sets.

In addition, limited signatures appear in CHL First Draft Auto (/15), In the Lab Auto (/15), Prominent Combos Auto (/15) and That’s a Rocket Auto (/15).

Estimated Release Date: May 22, 2025

2024-25 Upper Deck CHL Hockey Product Configuration

Hobby Boxes

  • 16 cards per pack; 12 packs per box; 20 boxes per case

Blaster Boxes

  • 8 cards per pack; 7 packs per box

2024-25 Upper Deck CHL Hockey Box Break

Hobby Boxes (on average)

  • 1 Autograph or Base Outburst Parallel
  • 2 #'d Parallels AND/OR Non-Auto Printing Plates
  • 12 Base Stars AND/OR Base Star Rookies Cards
  • 9 Inserts (CHL First Draft, In the Lab, Prominent Combos AND/OR That’s a Rocket)

Blaster Boxes (on average)

  • 1 Exclusive Green Parallel
  • 7 Base Stars AND/OR Base Star Rookies Cards
  • 1 In the Lab Inserts
  • 2 Additional Inserts (CHL First Draft, Prominent Combos AND/OR That’s a Rocket)
