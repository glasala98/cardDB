Led by Young Guns rookies and more, 2024-25 Upper Deck Series 1 Hockey marks the start of the flagship collecting season for NHL fans.

The flagship product keeps the revamped Hobby format from last year, including 12 packs per box with three inserts per pack.

In total, each Hobby box should contain six Young Guns and four UD Canvas cards, plus 21 additional inserts and four parallels. There is also one limited card that is either serial-numbered, a ratio short print or a printing plate.

Look for 2024-25 Upper Deck Series 2 Hockey and 2024-25 Upper Deck Extended Series Hockey later in the season.

2024-25 Upper Deck Series 1 Hockey Checklist Rundown

As the focal point of the product, the 2024-25 Upper Deck Series 1 Hockey checklist relies heavily on the Young Guns selection.

UD also revealed the expected continuation of cards as it applies to 2024-25 Upper Deck Series 2 Hockey and 2024-25 Upper Deck Extended Series Hockey.

Of course, the base and Young Guns are spread across all three series along with UD Canvas. This intra-series lineup also includes Dazzlers (all three series), O-Pee-Chee Glossy (Series 1 and Series 2), Population Count (Series 1 and Series 2), UD Portraits (Series 1 and Series 2) and Young Guns Renewed (Series 1 and Series 2).

2024-25 Upper Deck Series 1 Hockey Base & Parallel Details

Providing the first run of base cards, 2024-25 Upper Deck Series 1 Hockey features 200 veterans.

Members of the Arizona Coyotes are noted as Utah Hockey Club for the team affiliation, acknowledging the franchise's move.

Parallels include staples like UD Exclusives (/100) and High Gloss (/10). The Outburst editions are back, combining to fall once per Hobby box.

2024-25 Upper Deck Series 1 Hockey Insert Details

Beginning with the new content, 2024-25 Upper Deck Series 1 Hockey is packed with inserts. Exclusive to Hobby, the #1 Pick Redemption is a tough pull at 1:1,440 packs.

Other debuts include A-Lines (1:12.5 packs), covering three top stars from each team, Aimbots (1:6 packs), which highlights leading scorers by identifying the shots available to them, and Breaking News (1:6 packs), dedicated to key moments from 2023-24.

There's also Checkpoint (1:6 packs), looking at players who excel at checking, and City Satellites (1:7.5 packs), which displays the NHL's elite against a satellite image of their hometown.

The Gaming insert line is another new addition for 2024-25 Upper Deck Series 1 Hockey. The massive 100-card insert offers four different subsets: Gaming Co-Op (1:8 packs) for teammates, Gaming FOV (1:8 packs), with a Field of View image, Gaming PVP (1:8 packs), showcasing a head-to-head matchup, and Gaming XP (1:8 packs), which assigns an experience level for each player.

Boasting the return of short-printed hologram inserts, Holotypes (1:504) is a "living set" with no repeats. Filled with current superstars, the 30-card selection marks the start of a Series 1 insert expected to continue for multiple seasons.

Another option likely to see interest, Young Guns Renewed (1:4 packs) revisits popular YG cards of the past with an assist from artist Luke the Cardist. Per UD, "Series 1 will feature notable Young Guns up through the 2013-14 season."

That leaves the familiar lines in 2024-25 Upper Deck Series 1 Hockey, such as the colorful Dazzlers.

Population Count is back with cards limited to 1,000 copies or less and UD Portraits (1:5 packs) returns with a new look and new parallels.

In addition, Series 1 has the next 120-card installment of UD Canvas (1:3.5 packs) and UD Canvas Young Guns (1:24 packs).

The Sophomore Sensations inserts are only in the Starter Kit format.

2024-25 Upper Deck Series 1 Hockey Unannounced Content

Yet another Young Guns series comes from the Outburst Retro Young Guns super short print insert. The cards take old YG designs and add the new parallel finish. UD left these reprints off the 2024-25 Upper Deck Series 1 Hockey checklist as part of their typical unannounced content (aka Easter eggs), which means odds are not provided.

Crossed Up is also a new and unannounced insert line done in the style of cross stitching.

Fanimation is another unannounced insert, though it's seemingly in the flagship release every year. Other returning chases include Base Swagnificent image variations and Base Snow Spray Autographs.
