Just for retired players, 2024-25 Upper Deck Engrained Icons Hockey brings collectors another offering of the high-end NHL card brand.

Featuring top names from the NHL's Alumni Association, every box has three autographs or relics, with a focus on hockey stick pieces. This product is officially known as Volume I.

2024-25 Upper Deck Engrained Icons Hockey Checklist Rundown

Mixing things up compared to the standard Engrained NHL format, the 2024-25 Upper Deck Engrained Icons Hockey checklist only supplies fans with former stars of the league.

Base & Insert Details

A total of 100 names make the cut for the base set in 2024-25 Upper Deck Engrained Icons Hockey. Each box has two base cards.

Base parallels include Ebony (/199), Mahogany (/75) and Rosewood (1/1).

The parallels combine with the Carved in Time (/100) wood inserts to average one per box.

Autograph & Relic Details

That means the rest of the 2024-25 Upper Deck Engrained Icons Hockey checklist arrives as hits, including three autographs or relics per box.

The Base Autos line breaks down into Tier 1 (1:18), Tier 2 (1:6) and Tier 3/Tier 4 (1:3) groupings. Limited versions include Ebony (/49 or less), Mahogany (/10 or /3) and Rosewood (/5 or 1/1).

Other signed choices appear in Beehive Portrait Signatures, with Tier 2-4 combining to average 1:2 boxes and Tier 1 averaging 1:60 boxes. These also have Red Ink versions with just five copies.

More favorites in 2024-25 Upper Deck Engrained Icons Hockey include Carved in Time Signatures (1:750), with an autograph on engraved wood stock.

Pairing autograph and relic, Complete Sticks Signatures (/25) displays three pieces of game-used stick. Gold parallels have just five copies.

Of course, Signature Shots is back for more. Per UD, these cards showcase "an autograph on a piece of game-used equipment." The groupings include Tier 2-4 (1:3) and Tier 1 (1:12), with Red Ink (/35 or /15) and Green Ink (/5) parallels.

Leaning into the retro-themed design, the all-new Highlight Tapes series comes unsigned (/35) and signed (/10).

That's just the start of the memorabilia selection in 2024-25 Upper Deck Engrained Icons Hockey.

Cuts of Class is a debuting line with Singles (1:3), Duals (/49), Triples (/25) and Quads (/10).

The relics continue in Complete Sticks (/49), Remnants (/100, /65 or /49), Rough Cuts (/49) and Stickside Splinters (/25).

Finally, the Distinguishing Marks, Stick Nameplates and Dual Stick Nameplates sets supply one-of-one chases sure to see plenty of attention from collectors.
