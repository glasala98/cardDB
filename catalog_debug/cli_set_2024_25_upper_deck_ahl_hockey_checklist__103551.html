2024-25 Upper Deck AHL Hockey Checklist Guide

Checklist Insider EditorOct 7, 2025Dec 5, 2025
  1. Home
  2. Hockey Cards
  3. 2024-25 Hockey Cards
  4. 2024-25 Upper Deck AHL Hockey Checklist Guide

Release Date: May 7, 2025

Brands

2024-25 Upper Deck AHL Hockey digs into another American Hockey League season covering top NHL prospects from teams in the United States and Canada.

The Hobby output remains the same, with boxes yielding one autograph, two numbered parallels, 12 base Star Rookies and 12 inserts, on average.

  • 2025-26 Upper Deck AHL Hockey Checklist Guide.
  • 2023-24 Upper Deck AHL Hockey Checklist Guide.

2024-25 Upper Deck AHL Hockey Checklist Rundown

As usual, the 2024-25 Upper Deck AHL Hockey checklist taps the developmental league for rising talent.

Teased signers include Jonathan Lekkerimäki and Olle Lycksell.

2024-25 Upper Deck AHL Hockey Base & Parallel Details

The base set for 2024-25 Upper Deck AHL Hockey is again comprised of 100 veterans and 50 Star Rookies (1 per pack).

All 150 cards add the classic UD Exclusives (/100) and High Gloss (/10) parallels.

2024-25 Upper Deck AHL Hockey Insert Details

In addition, inserts fall once per pack in 2024-25 Upper Deck AHL Hockey.

Key prospects are featured in the all-new Bound for Stardom (1:2 packs), offering 25 cards.

Highlighting those known for their well-rounded game, Two-Way Play (1:5 packs) is another debuting set.

Tendys, averaging 1:4 packs, returns to showcase top goaltenders.

Insert parallels include limited Red (/75) and Gold (/10) versions.

2024-25 Upper Deck AHL Hockey Autograph Details

That leaves the 2024-25 Upper Deck AHL Hockey autographs, averaging one per Hobby box.

The Base Autograph (1:18 packs) and Base Star Rookies Autograph (1:72 packs) cards apply to select names from the main lineup.

Collectors can also find rare insert autographs via Bound for Stardom Auto (1:144 packs), Tendys Auto (1:240 packs) and Two-Way Play Auto (1:192 packs).

Estimated Release Date: May 7, 2025

2024-25 Upper Deck AHL Hockey Product Configuration

Hobby Boxes

  • 10 cards per pack; 12 packs per box; 24 boxes per case (2 inners)

2024-25 Upper Deck AHL Hockey Box Break

Hobby Boxes (on average)

  • 1 Autograph
  • 12 Base Star Rookies
  • 2 #'d Parallels
  • 12 Inserts (Bound for Stardom, Tendys AND/OR Two-Way Play)
