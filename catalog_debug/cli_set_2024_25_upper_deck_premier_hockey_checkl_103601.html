2024-25 Upper Deck Premier Hockey Checklist Rundown

The 2024-25 Upper Deck Premier Hockey checklist again spreads the coverage to the past, present and future of the game.

Per UD, "All rookie content includes players who qualified for rookie card status as of 1/2/25."

Base & Parallel Details

Three groupings with different rarities fill out the base set in 2024-25 Upper Deck Premier Hockey.

The 150-card lineup has Base Veterans (#1-90; /299), Base Greats (#91-100; /99) and Base Rookies (#101-150; /399).

Each card also adds Gold and Platinum (1/1) parallels. For the Veterans and Greats, the Gold version has 25 copies, but the Rookies set increases that to 199 copies.

Autograph Details

Of course, many of the base subjects have signed versions, as well. 2024-25 Upper Deck Premier Hockey features Base Veterans Auto (1:4), Base Greats Auto (1:125) and Base Rookies Auto (1:4) cards.

Other autographed cards include Premier Focus Auto (#'d), Premier Penmanship Autos (1:5) and the all-new Old Style Signatures (1:18), showcasing a colorful, retro design.

Autograph Patch Details

With one per box, autograph patches are a focal point of 2024-25 Upper Deck Premier Hockey, especially the rookie content.

The Acetate Rookie Auto Patch (RAP) cards check in at 249 or 99 copies, while the Horizontal Acetate Rookie Auto Patch editions are limited to 99 or 49 copies at the main level.

There are also Acetate Veteran Auto Patch cards numbered to 49 or less.

The autographed memorabilia continues with 2024 Premier Rookie Auto Jersey (/99 or /25), Premier Focus Auto Patch (#'d) and Premier Focus Auto Patch Rookies (/99 or less).

Debuts include First Opus Rookie Auto Jersey (/35), Grand Opus Auto Jersey (/15) and Viewpoints Auto Patch (/99).

Relic Details

That leaves only the unsigned relics, and 2024-25 Upper Deck Premier Hockey is packed with options.

Select names have Base Veterans Jersey (1:3), Base Greats Jersey (1:128) and Base Rookies Jersey (1:2) cards, plus limited parallels like Neckline (/3).

Other familiar lines in 2024-25 Upper Deck Premier Hockey include Premier Attractions Jersey (/99 or /49), Premier Gear Jersey (/49) and Premier Gear Rookies Jersey (/99).

Chase multi-relic cards in Premier Dual Jerseys (/99), Premier Triple Jerseys (/49), Premier Quad Jerseys (/25), Premier Mega Jerseys Duos (/49), Premier Mega Jerseys Trios (/25) and Premier Mega Jerseys Quads (/15).

Mega Patch is another favorite, offering limited Chest Logos, Commemorative Logos, Shoulder Logos and Sleeve Numbers swatches.

As usual, the Patch-Tacular set is home to some of the most impressive patches via Rookie Patch-Tacular (/25 or less) and Dual Rookie Patch-Tacular (/25 or less).

Premier Pieces is a new option to find a variety of swatches in 2024-25 Upper Deck Premier Hockey, such as Equipment Bag (/35), Helmet (/35), Hockey Sock (/35), Pants (/35) and Skates (/15) relics.

For more newness, check out First Opus Rookie Jersey (/149), Grand Opus Jersey (/149) and Premier Twineline (/25), which displays game-used net cord pieces from three notable games.
