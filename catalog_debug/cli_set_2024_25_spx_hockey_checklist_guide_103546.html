2024-25 SPx Hockey refreshes the Upper Deck NHL brand with a new format.

Moving away from the one-pack-per-box product of 2023-24 and earlier, Hobby boxes yield eight packs with three cards each.

According to the details from UD, these boxes no longer average an autograph, with the expected output being a mix of parallels and inserts.

Although UD Black was a frequent accessory in recent iterations of SPx, that is no longer the case.

2024-25 SPx Hockey Checklist Rundown

While rare autographs are in play, the 2024-25 SPx Hockey checklist leans more on the inserts with an assortment of styles available.

2024-25 SPx Hockey Base & Parallel Details

Unlike 2023-24 SPx, which saw base print runs of 299 copies, 2024-25 SPx Hockey makes the base cards more accessible with one per pack from the 100-card lineup. Occupying the next 50 cards in the set (#101-150), the Base Rookie cards fall in every other pack.

There are also 10 Extended Rookies (1:12 packs) and 15 Rookie Holograms (1:86 packs).

The parallels should combine to offer three Silver cards and one Gold card per box.

2024-25 SPx Hockey Base Parallels List & Odds

  • Silver (Vets - 1:4 packs; Rookies - 1:10 packs; Extended Rookies - 1:48 packs)
  • Gold (Vets - 1:12 packs; Rookies - 1:30 packs; Extended Rookies - 1:144 packs)
  • Radiance - /100
  • Grand Finale - /50
  • Silver Spectrum - /25
  • Gold Spectrum - 1/1

2024-25 SPx Hockey Insert Details

Showcasing the typical SPx style, 2024-25, SPx Hockey supplies fans with a tech-heavy lineup of inserts.

Each box also has four cards from the PETG/Simulated Hologram grouping. This includes Bound For Glory (1:8 packs), Excellence Redefined (1:20 packs), Game Film (1:13 packs) and Holoview F/X (Rookies - 1:7 packs; Vets - 1:10 packs).

Finite Vets (/999) and Finite Rookies (/999), plus parallels (/599 or less), should provide another two cards per box.

Those chasing the tougher pulls have All-Time Holoview (1:40 packs), Force (Vet - 1:160 packs; Rookies - 1:267 packs) and SPxCellence (1:100 packs).

Digging into UD's past, Salute (1:800 packs) makes its SPx debut with a new look. In addition, Holo’d Hardware (1:267 packs) is also new.

2024-25 SPx Hockey Autograph Details

Autographs keep the hits rolling in 2024-25 SPx Hockey, including serial-numbered Base Rookie Autos.

Find top NHL stars and rookies among the Finite Silver Spectrum Auto (/99 or less) cards.

Limited versions arrive in Holographs (1:240 packs), Star Focus Signatures (1:270 packs), Superscripts (1:160 packs) and Rookie Superscripts (1:120 packs).

The signed choices continue with All-Time Holoview Autos (/10), Force Autos (/99 or less), Holo’d Hardware Auto (#'d) and SPxCellence Autos (#'d).

Finally, SPx nostalgia is on full display with 1996-97 SPx Buyback Autos (/13 or less).
